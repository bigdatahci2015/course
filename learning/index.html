Learning Challenges

  • Flipped classroom: You will do your learning not in the classroom but outside the classroom at your own preferred place (e.g., library, dorm room, coffee shop ...etc).
  • Learn in a team: Each week, the first half of the learning challenge requires you to work together with your team. The due date is always Friday 11:59pm.
  • Learn individually: The second half of the learning challenge requires you to tackle it on your own. The due date is always Sunday 11:59pm.
